# Code of Conduct

ZenFlare aims to be a calm, focused project: *No noise, just flow.* We expect everyone to contribute to a respectful and inclusive environment.

---

## Our standards

- Be respectful and inclusive.
- Welcome constructive feedback and different viewpoints.
- Focus on the code and ideas, not on personal attacks.
- No harassment, trolling, or discriminatory behavior.

---

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies to project spaces (repos, issues, PRs, discussions) and to public communication when representing ZenFlare.

---

## Enforcement

Maintainers may remove comments, close issues/PRs, or block users who violate these standards. For serious or repeated violations, we may take further action.

---

## Reporting

If you experience or witness unacceptable behavior, please report it by opening an issue (and marking it as sensitive if needed) or by contacting the maintainers. We will handle reports confidentially where appropriate.
